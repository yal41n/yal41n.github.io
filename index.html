Why It Matters

Security teams are flying blind

Most organizations discover security gaps only after an incident — or buried inside a static spreadsheet that's outdated before it's finished.

Manual & error-prone

Gap assessments done in spreadsheets take weeks, go stale immediately, and miss critical control areas due to human oversight.

No prioritization

Without context-aware risk scoring, teams waste effort on low-impact findings while critical exposure points go unaddressed for months.

Siloed teams

Security, IT, and compliance work in disconnected tools. Accountability gaps form, remediation stalls, and audits become last-minute fire drills.

Confidentiality risks

Sending sensitive security posture data to cloud-only vendors creates regulatory exposure and contradicts the principle of least privilege.

How It Works

From gaps to remediation in five steps

GAPLIZZER's multi-agent AI orchestrates your entire GAP analysis lifecycle — so your team focuses on decisions, not data collection.

1

Connect & scope

Connect your environment (APIs, agents, or manual import) and define assessment scope against chosen frameworks.

2

AI-driven gap detection

Specialized AI agents scan controls, evidence, and configurations to surface gaps mapped to ISO 27001, NIST CSF, and SOC 2.

3

Prioritize by risk

The virtual CISO scores and ranks gaps by business impact, likelihood, and regulatory weight — so you act on what matters.

4

Collaborate & assign

Shared workspace lets your team assign owners, set deadlines, attach evidence, and track remediation in real time.

5

Close & verify

AI validates closure evidence, updates your posture score, and automatically re-maps gaps for continuous monitoring.
